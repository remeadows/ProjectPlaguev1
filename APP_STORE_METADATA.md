**GRID WATCH ZERO: NEURAL GRID**

You are a grey-hat data broker operating in the shadows of the digital underground. Your mission: harvest data, grow your network, and survive the relentless attacks of Malus - a rogue AI that has marked you for termination.

**STRATEGIC IDLE GAMEPLAY**
• Build a data pipeline: Sources generate data, Links transfer it, Sinks convert it to credits
• Manage bandwidth bottlenecks and optimize your network flow
• Upgrade your infrastructure across 6 tiers of increasingly powerful units
• Balance offense and defense as threats escalate

**DEEP DEFENSE SYSTEMS**
• Deploy firewalls, intrusion detection, and AI-powered countermeasures
• 6 security categories with progressive unlock chains
• Track Malus attack patterns with Intelligence gathering
• React to critical threats with strategic defense deployment

**20-LEVEL CAMPAIGN**
• Progress from protecting a simple home network to defending reality itself
• Each level introduces new mechanics and challenges
• Insane mode for hardcore players seeking the ultimate challenge
• Story moments featuring memorable characters: Rusty, Tish, Fl3x, and the mysterious Helix

**RICH PROGRESSION**
• 30+ milestones and achievements to unlock
• Collect lore fragments revealing the world's dark secrets
• Prestige system with Helix Cores for permanent bonuses
• Cosmetic unlocks: UI themes and node skins

**CYBERPUNK AESTHETIC**
• Terminal-inspired UI with neon accents
• Dynamic threat indicators and attack visualizations
• Atmospheric sound design

Face the darkness. Find Helix. Survive.

## App Review Notes

This is a single-player strategy idle game. No network features require authentication.

The game auto-saves progress locally and optionally syncs to iCloud.

Key features for review:
1. Start the app -> Title screen -> Main Menu
2. Select "New Game" to start campaign
3. Level 1 "Home Protection" is the tutorial
4. Tap units to view stats, tap upgrade buttons to progress
5. Shop button (cart icon) opens unit store
6. Intel button (book icon) shows collected lore

No in-app purchases or subscriptions in this version.
