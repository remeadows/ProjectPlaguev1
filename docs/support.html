Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved to your device. If you have iCloud enabled, it will also sync across your Apple devices.

Why isn't my progress syncing between devices?

Make sure iCloud is enabled on both devices:

  • Go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud
  • Ensure iCloud Drive is turned ON
  • Check that Grid Watch Zero is enabled under "Apps Using iCloud"

How do I reset my game progress?

To reset all progress, delete and reinstall the app. To also clear iCloud data, go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage → Grid Watch Zero → Delete Data.

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have enough free storage space

How do I unlock new units?

Progress through the campaign to unlock higher-tier units. Each level introduces new equipment and defense applications.

What is Insane Mode?

Insane Mode is an optional harder difficulty available after completing a level on normal mode. It features 2x threat frequency, 1.5x damage, and 0.75x income.
