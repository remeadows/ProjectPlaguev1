Privacy Policy

Grid Watch Zero: Neural Grid

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Overview

Grid Watch Zero is developed by War Signal. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information We Collect

Grid Watch Zero does not collect any personal information:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers for tracking
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not display advertisements

Data Storage

The App stores game progress in two locations:

  • Local Storage: Game progress is saved on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud sync.
  • iCloud Sync (Optional): If iCloud is enabled, game progress syncs across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store, encrypted and managed by Apple.

We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

Grid Watch Zero may use these Apple services:

  • iCloud: Optional game progress synchronization
  • Game Center: Optional achievements and leaderboards

These are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Grid Watch Zero is rated 9+ and suitable for children. We do not collect personal information from any users. No account creation or personal data entry is required.

Data Deletion

To remove local game data:

  • Delete the App from your device
  • For iCloud data: Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage → Grid Watch Zero
